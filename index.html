Why 50,000+ Global Buyers Trust Fishgoo as Their China Shopping Agent

Fishgoo is a professional China purchasing agent that helps international buyers source products from Chinese e-commerce platforms including Taobao, Tmall, 1688, Pinduoduo, and JD.com. Since 2019, we have served over 50,000 customers across 200+ countries, processing more than 1 million parcels with a 99% safe delivery rate.

Whether you are an e-commerce entrepreneur looking to source inventory, a dropshipper seeking reliable suppliers, or an individual buyer wanting to access products only available in China, Fishgoo provides end-to-end service — from product sourcing and supplier verification, through quality control and warehouse storage, to international shipping with full package tracking. Our bilingual support team is available 24/7 to assist with any questions about customs, shipping routes, or product selection.

The Workflow

Sourcing made simple

shopping_cart

1. Find & Order

Simply paste product links from Taobao, Tmall, or 1688 into your Fishgoo dashboard. Our bilingual procurement agents negotiate directly with Chinese suppliers, verify product authenticity, and place orders on your behalf — typically within 24 hours of submission.

fact_check

2. QC & Store

Once items arrive at our Guangzhou warehouse, we conduct thorough quality inspection and send high-definition photos to your dashboard. Enjoy up to 90 days of complimentary storage while you wait for all orders to arrive before shipping.

local_shipping

3. Ship to You

Consolidate multiple orders into one package to save up to 80% on international shipping costs. We partner with DHL, FedEx, UPS, and EMS to offer door-to-door delivery to over 200 countries with full tracking and insurance options.
